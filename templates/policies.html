  • You must be at least 18 years old to use RentIt

  • You agree to provide accurate and complete information
  • You will not use the service for any illegal or unauthorized purpose
  • You are responsible for all activities under your account

3. Rental Agreement

  • All rentals are subject to availability and owner approval
  • Rental prices are clearly displayed and include applicable taxes
  • You agree to return items in the same condition as received
  • Late returns may incur additional charges
  • Damage or loss of items will be charged to your account

4. Security Deposit

A security deposit is required for all rentals. The deposit will be:

  • Held during the rental period
  • Refunded within 3-5 business days after item return
  • Used to cover damages or cleaning fees if necessary
  • Partially or fully forfeited in case of item loss or severe damage

5. Payment Terms

  • You authorize RentIt to charge your payment method for all fees

RentIt acts as a platform connecting renters and owners. We are not responsible for:

  • Quality, safety, or legality of items listed

If an owner cancels your confirmed booking:

  • We will help you find alternative items

  • Price differences will be charged or refunded accordingly
  • Contact customer support for modification requests

Emergency Cancellations

In case of emergencies (medical, natural disasters, etc.), contact our support team. We review each case individually and may provide exceptions to our standard policy.
